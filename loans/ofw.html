OFW LOAN

CRITERIA

To be granted to Overseas Filipino Workers (OFWs) to finance his placement fee and other necessary expenses in securing working documents.

TERM, INTEREST AND PROCESSING FEE OF LOAN
  • Max. of 1 year
  • 15% Interest
  • 3% Processing Fee
REQUIREMENTS/CONDITIONS
  • Photocopy of passport
  • Visa and contract
  • Acceptable collateral
MODE OF PAYMENT
  • Monthly
  • Quarterly
REMARKS

Grace period of 3 months
