COOP LOAN

CRITERIA

A loan window to cater the needs of Bankâ€™s members to finance their operation.

    Borrowers Eligibility Criteria
  • Must be a regular member of Ilocos Consolidated Cooperative Bank and/or cooperative with Due for Deposit on Stock Subscription (DSS) with approved membership from the board
  • With current Certificate of Compliance from the Cooperative Development Authority and the Bank
TERM, INTEREST AND PROCESSING FEE OF LOAN
  • Max. of 1 year
  • 8% interest and 2% processing fee
REQUIREMENTS/CONDITIONS
  • Post dated checks covering installment payments (case to case basis)
  • Certified photocopy of the Articles of Cooperation and By-laws and amendments, if any (for new applicants)
  • photocopy of CDA Certificate of Registration (for new applicants)
  • Copy of audited financial statements for the past two (2) years
  • Latest interim financial statements
  • Board resolution requesting for financial assistance and designating at least two (2) authorized representatives
  • List of key officers
  • ID photo of authorized representative
  • Certificate of Compliance from Cooperative Development Authority
  • Acceptable collaterals, if needed
  • All credit accommodation to DOSRI regardless of amount shall follow the DOSRI and RPT Policies (refer to Annex A)

  • Quarterly

  • Lumpsum
