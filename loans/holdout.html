HOLDOUT LOAN

CRITERIA

Secured by hold-out deposit(s) either time or savings deposit. The applicant/borrower must be a depositor of ICCB.

TERM, INTEREST AND PROCESSING FEE OF LOAN
  • Interest rate of deposit plus 3% mark-up
  • Term depends on the term of the deposit but max. of 1 year
  • No processing fee
REQUIREMENTS/CONDITIONS
  • The original copy of the certificate of time deposit (CTD) or savings passbook subject to the hold-out or assignment shall be surrendered to the Bank, and a duly notarized deed of assignment or hold-out agreement in favor of the bank shall be duly executed by the borrower
  • If the term of the CTD subject to hold out or assignment is shorter than the term of the loan, there shall be agreement in writing that the renewal of time deposit upon maturity shall coincide with the term of the loan
  • No pre-termination of the time deposit unless the loan is fully paid or acceptable substitute collateral is presented
MODE OF PAYMENT

Lump-sum

REMARKS
  • Agricultural Facility Acquisitions – quarterly, semi-annual
  • Production – lumpsum
  • Swine Fattening - 6 months – lumpsum
  • Cattle breeding – 2 to 3 years
  • Poultry(broiler) – quarterly
  • Egg layering – monthly
  • Goat raising – 1 year lumpsum
  • Fisheries – 1 year, quarterly, bi-annual, lumpsum
  • Facilities on Fisheries – max. of 3 years, monthly
  • Cattle Fattening – 1 to 3 years
  • Breeding – 3 years
  • Specific services with corresponding loan range and term
  • Unsecured – 500,000.00 unsecured (depending on the set-up policy in granting such amount)
