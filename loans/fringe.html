FRINGE BENEFIT LOAN

CRITERIA

Financial assistance to the employees of the bank in the form of loans at preferential rate and affordable installment plan. Changes of the plan require prior BSP approval. (refer to Annex “L” for the complete guidelines).

TERM, INTEREST AND PROCESSING FEE OF LOAN
  • All FB Loans Interest Rate 5% except Motorcycle loan at 0% and emergency loan at 3%

  • Housing – 15 years
