GSIS/SSS/PVAO PENSION LOAN

CRITERIA

To provide financial assistance to pensioners for their income generating projects and providential purposes.

    The borrower must be:
  • GSIS/SSS/PVAO Pensioner
  • Not more than 69 years old upon maturity of the loan
TERM, INTEREST AND PROCESSING FEE OF LOAN
  • Max. of 1 year
  • 15% Interest
  • 3% Processing Fee
REQUIREMENTS/CONDITIONS
  • Three (3) months bank statement (account were the pension is being credited or subject to deed of assignment)
  • Duly signed deed of assignment
MODE OF PAYMENT

Payable monthly or it will depend on the schedule of the pensioner’s pension
