SALARY LOAN

CRITERIA

Eligible borrowers are permanent employees of Private/Government Agencies within the bank’s area of business operation.

    Institution
  1. Letter of intent to avail salary loan
  2. Memorandum of agreement (MOA)
  3. For LGU – SB Resolution to authorize the municipal mayor and treasurer to be their official signatories to the bank
  4. For Private agency – Board of Directors resolution to authorize official signatories
  5. Photocopy of general payroll of all officers and employees of the agency
TERM, INTEREST AND PROCESSING FEE OF LOAN
  • Max. of 5 years, 15%, and 3% processing fee
  • For new loans (new client) – 3 years
REQUIREMENTS/CONDITIONS
  • Certified photocopy of appointment and/or service record for government/private employee and oath of office for elected officials
  • Certified photocopy of latest payroll and original copy of payslip
  • Duly accomplished loan application
  • Photocopy of two (2) Valid IDs
  • 2 copies of Photo ID 2x2
  • Other required documents, if any
MODE OF PAYMENT
  • Monthly
