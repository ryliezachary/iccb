AGRICULTURAL LOAN

CRITERIA

Agricultural Credit in general shall include all loans granted to borrowers, whether beneficiaries of agrarian reform or not, to finance activities relating to agriculture and for processing, marketing, storage and distribution of products resulting from these agricultural activities.

TERM, INTEREST AND PROCESSING FEE OF LOAN
  • Interest Rate of 15% (except special program)
  • Processing fee of 3% per availment
  • Depending on the project to be financed – max. of 5 years.
REQUIREMENTS/CONDITIONS
  • Farm Plan/Project Proposal
  • Quotation/Deliver Receipt
  • Business Permit
  • Environmental Compliance Certificate (ECC)
MODE OF PAYMENT

Depends upon the source of payment of the borrower and his project.

REMARKS
  • Agricultural Facility Acquisitions – quarterly, semi-annual
  • Production – lumpsum
  • Swine Fattening - 6 months – lumpsum
  • Cattle breeding – 2 to 3 years
  • Poultry(broiler) – quarterly
  • Egg layering – monthly
  • Goat raising – 1 year lumpsum
  • Fisheries – 1 year, quarterly, bi-annual, lumpsum
  • Facilities on Fisheries – max. of 3 years, monthly
  • Cattle Fattening – 1 to 3 years
  • Breeding – 3 years
  • Specific services with corresponding loan range and term
  • Unsecured – 500,000.00 unsecured (depending on the set-up policy in granting such amount)
